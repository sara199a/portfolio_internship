About

Here you will find out more about a former schoolteacher, who loves to create tufted rugs as her side hustle. You will also find a personalitytest and a overwiev of my technical skills. I hope you will enjoy.

A few words

An educated primary school teacher, who, after two years of work, had to explore her creative side.

Creativity

My creativity comes in many ways such as tufting, where I work with soft textiles and create rugs for both walls and floors.

I also love to dive into different DIY projects. Recently I've started knitting, but I also love painting, building small furnitures for my home and creating cute little trinkets such as colorful candles.

In addition to the crafts, I also enjoy the digital creative work I get to do as a multimedia designer. I'm in my element when the creative is combined with knowledge and problem solving.

Where to find me

I live in Vesterbro and enjoy the city's many opportunities. At the same time, I also love to disappear into the nature. Otherwise you can find me in my tufting studio, aka bedroom, where I create handmade rugs.

Sports have always been a part of my spare time, where I have done everything from gymnastics to crossfit. Life requires balance. Therefore you will also be able to find me at the local bar with a beer or at home drinking wine, laughing and having deep conversations with my dear friends.

A creative journey

In December 2021, I took my first step on my creative journey, starting with tufting. It took nearly four months, and then my works were in the first shop in Copenhagen. That wasn't actually the plan, as I started tufting for myself. But when you find something that you really passionate about, things can move quickly.

Tufting is a space for me to find peace and focus through the craft. In my designs, I'm particularly interested in colors, shapes, realism and abstract. Through my tufted works, I hope to bring more color and joy to everyday life.
