Vildskud Festival

Own role

Design, UX, UI and graphics.

Radical redesign of website

This was my first project in my 3rd semester, where I had chosen digital design as an elective.

In this project, we had to create a radical redesign for Vildskud Festival, who wanted a modern, creative and trustworthy universe. Furthermore, the customer wanted a sustainable site, which was why we, in the group, gave ourselves a constraint - no pictures. Instead, I drew all graphics in Illustrator.

The primary focus was on the organization's visual identity, the festival program and the ticket page, as our target group was the festival goer. Throughout the designphase, I tested numerous user flows and worked according to the "don't make me think" principle.
